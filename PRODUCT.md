# GEMRITE — Precious Stone Authentication Platform

## What It Is
GEMRITE delivers instant certified authenticity verification for precious stones at the point of sale. Users submit a stone and receive a certified verification result in seconds — with fraud and counterfeit detection built in.

## Target Users
- **Buyers** — individuals purchasing precious stones who need confidence in authenticity before committing
- **Sellers / Jewelers / Dealers** — professionals who need to certify stones to close sales with trust
- **Personal Collectors** — enthusiasts who want verified records of their collection

## Core First Feature
Instant stone authentication: submit a stone, receive a certified verification result in seconds.

## Pricing
- **Pay-as-you-go:** $9 per verification — no commitment, pay only when you need it
- **Pro Plan:** $79/month — unlimited verifications + PDF certificate of authenticity included

## Brand Voice & Tone
Authoritative, precise, and trustworthy. GEMRITE is not a generic appraisal service — it is purpose-built for precious stones. The experience should feel premium, fast, and institutional.

## Strategic Principles
1. **Speed is the trust mechanism** — results in seconds, not days; verification happens at the exact moment of sale
2. **Certificate-backed results** — Pro Plan users receive a PDF certificate of authenticity
3. **Fraud/counterfeit detection** — built-in, not bolted on
4. **Purpose-built** — designed exclusively for precious stones, not generic jewelry or appraisals
