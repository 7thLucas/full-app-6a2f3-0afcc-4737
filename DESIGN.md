# GEMRITE Design Guidelines

## Color Palette
- **Background (Primary):** Dark navy `#0f172a`
- **Background (Surface):** `#1e293b` (slate-800 equivalent)
- **Accent / CTA:** Gold/amber `#f59e0b`
- **Accent Hover:** `#d97706`
- **Text Primary:** `#f8fafc` (near white)
- **Text Secondary:** `#94a3b8` (slate-400)
- **Success:** `#22c55e`
- **Error/Fraud:** `#ef4444`
- **Border:** `#334155` (slate-700)

## Typography
- **Display / Headlines:** Serif or high-contrast sans-serif — conveys authority and permanence (e.g., Playfair Display or similar)
- **Body / UI:** Clean sans-serif — precise and readable (e.g., Inter)
- **Certificate Text:** Monospace or formal serif for certificate-style outputs

## Elevation & Depth
- Dark layered surfaces: navy base → slate surface → card overlays
- Subtle gold borders or glows on interactive elements and verified results
- No heavy drop shadows — use border and background contrast for depth

## Components
- **Hero Section:** Full-width, dark navy, gold headline accent, single strong CTA ("Verify a Stone")
- **Verification Form/Card:** Centered card on dark surface, clean input fields, gold submit button
- **Result Card:** Certified badge (gold checkmark or seal), stone details, pass/fail indicator
- **Pricing Cards:** Two-tier, pay-as-you-go vs Pro, gold highlight on Pro
- **Certificate Preview:** Document-style card mimicking a formal certificate (Pro Plan)
- **Navigation:** Minimal top nav, logo left, links right, gold CTA button

## Overall Aesthetic
Premium, institutional, and precise. Think: a luxury authentication house meets a modern SaaS dashboard. Clean layouts, confident whitespace, gold accents used sparingly for maximum impact. No playful or casual elements.
